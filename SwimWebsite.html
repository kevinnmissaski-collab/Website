About Us

Hello! This is the official website for the RSC Swimming Club.
The Club was founded in 1949 and is available to people of all ages, no matter their ability.
Whatever your ability or interest, from beginner to highly trained competetive swimmers, we have a squad for you!

All of our squads are trained using friendly ASA Qualified Coaches, who would be pleased to discuss your needs and tailor the experience accordingly!
Don't be shy, drop on by and join the fun, we offer training and courses for everyone,
We like our community to be fun and inclusive.

Competitive Races  

 Our races are competitive event where athletes swim a set distance using specific strokes—freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, or butterfly—aiming to be the fastest to the finish, either in a pool (50m long course or 25m short course) or in open water. Races range from short sprints to long-distance, with winners decided by time.

Backstroke 

Backstroke is one of the four main competitive swimming styles.  It involves alternating straight-arm recoveries over the water, a pulling motion underneath, and a continuous, high-rate "flutter kick" to maintain speed while breathing easily.
